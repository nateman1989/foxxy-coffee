Barista's Corner: Tasty Brew Tips
  • Buy fresh, whole bean coffee
  • Coffee reaches its peak flavor just days after being roasted
  • Store the coffee properly
  • Store in a mason jar or vacuum sealed container
  • Grind your coffee immediately before roasting
  • Achieve a medium-medium fine grind with a manual hand mill
  • Pre-infuse coffee grounds by pouring hot water over coffee grounds before using coffee maker
